Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Laina is a personal agreement tracking service operated by Aris Digital Ltd. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

What we collect

  • Phone numbers — yours and the person you create an agreement with, so we can send messages and match records
  • Display name — your WhatsApp profile name, to personalise messages
  • Agreement details — amount, number of payments, frequency, notes, and status
  • Timestamps — when agreements are created, confirmed, and updated

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your ID, address, date of birth, or financial account details
  • We do not read your other WhatsApp messages
  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not process or move money

Why we collect it

We use your data solely to provide the Laina service: recording agreements, sending confirmations, tracking payments, and sending reminders. We do not use your personal data for advertising.

Anonymised analytics

We collect anonymised, aggregated statistics about how Laina is used — such as the number of agreements created per week or average agreement size by region. This data contains no personally identifiable information and cannot be traced back to any individual.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored in secure, encrypted databases hosted by Supabase (Google Cloud infrastructure). All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Every agreement event is secured with cryptographic protections to prevent tampering.

Data retention

We retain your agreement data for as long as you have active agreements. Completed agreements are retained for 24 months to allow for evidence report generation. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

Under applicable data protection legislation, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

Third parties

We use the following third-party services to operate Laina:

  • Meta (WhatsApp Cloud API) — to send and receive WhatsApp messages
  • Supabase — to store agreement data securely
  • Railway — to host our backend application
  • Stripe — to process payments for evidence reports (only if you purchase a report)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

Children

Laina is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via WhatsApp.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to exercise your data rights, contact us at hello@aris-digital.com.
