How it works

Three steps. No app to download.

01

Record the agreement

Message Laina on WhatsApp with the details — who, how much, and how many payments. Takes 30 seconds.

02

They confirm

The other person gets a message to confirm. Once they agree, both of you have a timestamped, verified record.

03

Laina keeps track

Gentle reminders before each payment day. Mark payments as done. Both sides see the same record, always.

Built for trust

Your agreements. Your data. Protected.

Laina doesn't move money or process payments. We simply keep a clear, honest record of what two people agreed on.

🔒

Tamper-proof records

Every event is cryptographically secured. If anyone tries to change a record, it's immediately detectable — keeping your agreements honest and verifiable.

🕐

Timestamped everything

Creation, confirmation, payments, reminders — every action is logged with an exact timestamp. Nothing can be backdated.

🤝

Both sides confirm

An agreement only becomes active when both people confirm. Nobody can create a one-sided claim.

🛡️

Minimal data, maximum privacy

We collect only what's needed — phone numbers and agreement details. No unnecessary personal data. No selling your information. Ever.

We don't replace trust between people. We just make sure everyone remembers what they agreed on.
